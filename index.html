Bernhard, currently employed as a PhD fellow at the University of Copenhagen, has a focus on understanding and modeling human spatial behavior.

With a theoretical and practical background in planning, (landscape) architecture, geographic information science, computer science, systems design and planning communications, Bernhard is as well intrigued by designing, developing and implementing of agent-based models, spatial decision support systems and planning support systems.

Over more than a decade, Bernhard acted as a consultant for private clients as well as public instances and universities.

Bernhard has been working nationally, as well internationally and abroad in areas of humanitarian crises.

Bernhard tries to approach projects holistically from phases of early idea conception until the final publication.

Bernhard loves to create and share code
